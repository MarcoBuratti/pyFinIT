StockBOT has three main functions:
- <u>Tracking</u>, says how well the individual stocks and portfolio performed in the past year
- <u>Forecast</u>, try to make a forecast for the next five years of your portfolio
- <u>Analysis</u>, through the CAPM and Markowitz tries to give the measure of the best portfolio possible
